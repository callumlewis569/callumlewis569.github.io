Our Services

We offer various high quality services including:

Accident/Insurance

We cover day to day knocks and bumps to estimates for insurance companies.

Lease/PCP

Do you have a vehicle coming to the end of its contract? We can give you an estimate on any work needed for its return.

Wheels/Calipers

We offer standard wheel repairs, diamond cut wheel repairs, and custom colours and finishes for break calipers.

Custom Paintwork

We can offer all special effect paints to achieve your dream look.

Restorations

We offer full shell repaints or bare metal shell repaints and are happy to work with your budget to get your project looking good as new.
